The Meta-Agreement

Perhaps the strongest common ground is that both sides want America to be a place where hardworking people can build good lives for themselves and their families. They disagree on the threats and solutions, but they share the fundamental goal of a thriving, prosperous, and secure America.

While deep ideological differences remain, these areas of potential common ground suggest that productive cooperation isn't impossible.
