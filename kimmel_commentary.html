We Found Something on the Internet

A scholarly examination of contemporary American devotional art

Okay, so someone sent me this. Apparently there is now a genre of AI-generated painting where Donald Trump is depicted as Jesus Christ, and I want to be very clear — I did not make this up. This is a real thing that real adults have chosen to create and, presumably, hang somewhere near a couch. It has bald eagles, a fighter jet, the Statue of Liberty, a man being healed in a hospital bed, ascending soldiers, and fireworks. It's like someone typed "America maximum" into a computer and this came out. Let's take a closer look.

Fig. 1 — "The Second Coming, But Make It Electoral" — AI-generated, artist unknown, provenance: someone's Facebook feed, 2024
↑ Point 1 — The Subject
The central figure wears white robes and a red sash — the traditional garments of a messiah, or, based on the tie length, a very casual Mar-a-Lago Tuesday.
↑ Point 2 — The Eagles
Two bald eagles flank the scene. The artist has made them roughly the size of Buicks. In real life, bald eagles weigh about 14 pounds. This is not that.
↑ Point 3 — The Healing
The central miracle: laying hands on a sick man with a glowing golden light. Notably, this is the only known depiction of Trump near a hospital where he appears to be helping someone.
↑ Point 4 — The Fighter Jets
At least three F-16s are present at what appears to be a miracle. Biblical scholars have searched the texts. The jets are not in there.
↑ Point 5 — The Nurse
A nurse watches from the corner with an expression of cautious professional concern. Same expression, honestly, as when the real administration handled healthcare policy.
↑ Point 6 — The Ascending Soldiers
Glowing figures rise toward heaven in the upper center. This is either a rapture scene or a very optimistic rendering of the Space Force recruitment poster.
The Bit

"The artist clearly asked the AI for 'most American thing possible' and then hit generate six more times until they got enough eagles."
On the religious iconography
"Jesus famously said 'blessed are the meek.' The man in this painting has two eagles and a fighter jet. Slightly different vibe."
On the AI generation
"You can tell it's AI because the hands almost look right. Almost. Almost. Keep staring at the fingers. Sleep tight."
On the fireworks

On the Statue of Liberty
"The Statue of Liberty is visible in the background. She appears to be watching very cautiously — which, frankly, is relatable."
On the target market
"This is being sold somewhere as a commemorative tapestry. I don't know where. I don't want to know where. And yet, somehow, I know."

"Look — I'm not here to tell anyone what to put in their home. That's between you, God, and the AI company that made this. This is America. That's their right. God bless 'em. Every single one."
