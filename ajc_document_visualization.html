Document Overview

This AJC document responds to accusations that Israel is committing genocide in Gaza following Hamas's October 7, 2023 attack. The document argues that such claims are "inaccurate, misleading, and dangerous" and presents a legal and factual defense of Israel's military response.

1 Legal Definition of Genocide

Core Argument: Genocide requires intent to destroy a group based on identity, not individual actions. Israel's war is against Hamas, not Palestinians as a group.

References the 1948 UN Genocide Convention and Raphael Lemkin's original definition. Emphasizes that genocide targets group identity, not military/political actions.

2 Response to Genocidal Attack

Core Argument: Israel is responding to Hamas's genocidal intentions and October 7 massacre, which targeted civilians based on their identity.

Hamas has explicitly stated goals to eliminate Israel and Jews. Israel's military response is justified under international law as self-defense.

3 Efforts to Protect Civilians

Core Argument: Israel's actions demonstrate intent to minimize civilian harm, contradicting genocidal intent.

Examples include advance warnings, evacuation corridors, humanitarian aid deliveries, and targeting military infrastructure rather than civilians.

4 Hamas's Use of Human Shields

Core Argument: Hamas deliberately endangers Palestinian civilians to blame Israel and gain public opinion advantage.

Hamas operates from civilian areas, uses human shields, and transforms protected sites into legitimate military targets under international law.

5 Factual Uncertainties

Core Argument: Claims of genocide are based on unverified information from Hamas-controlled sources.

Key unknowns include: actual civilian vs. combatant casualties, circumstances of deaths, and verification of military objectives at attack sites.

Document Conclusion

The document argues that genocide accusations against Israel are both legally unfounded and factually unsupported. It contends that responsibility for Palestinian suffering lies with Hamas, and that efforts should focus on defeating Hamas to achieve peace for both Palestinians and Israelis.
