Breaking Down Complexity Without Dumbing Down

Example: TikTok ownership explained through the absurdity of two men in their 80s and 90s running a youth social media platform. His kids being "super pumped about the new guys in charge" instantly conveys the disconnect while teaching who controls TikTok and why it matters.

Making Abstraction Concrete

Example: Media consolidation listed out - "Rupert Murdoch and Larry Ellison in charge of TikTok, Elon Musk running X, Mark Zuckerberg has Facebook..." Then the punchline about "left-wing libtards control everything." Audience laughs at the irony while registering actual concentration of media power.

Using Ridicule to Reveal Truth

Example: "Another word for frozen escalator is stairs" gets a laugh but exposes political theater and manufactured crises around portraying strength while treating minor inconveniences as threats.
