üìùAfterwords: The Innovation

This unique feature sets our archives apart:

What It Is

A curated summary of topics from online news publications that were actually discussed during the live session, derived directly from the Zoom transcript.

  • Transcript-Derived: Automatically identifies topics discussed from the recording
  • Source Linking: Connects to original news articles that were mentioned
  • Session Follow-up: Allows members to review items post-discussion
  • Coverage Tracking: Shows what was and wasn't covered during the live session
  • Additional Research: Provides access to news items that may not have been fully discussed due to time constraints
