I.

Teaching Objectives & Core Concepts

🔤
Foundation — Module 1
How Generative AI Works: Token by Token
Demonstrated the single-word prediction loop — each token drawn from 50,000 ranked candidates — to show how LLMs are fundamentally statistical pattern engines, not reasoning minds.
"Every time it does that, it is estimated it takes about $3.5 trillion calculations — that's at the very root of the energy problem AI poses."
🌐
Foundation — Module 2
RAG: The Leap from Frozen Scholar to Live Research Partner
Explained Retrieval Augmented Generation as the critical capability that makes AI relevant for current events — enabling access to today's published information rather than a frozen training corpus.
"RAG transforms AI from a well-trained but temporarily frozen scholar into a live research partner."
⚖️
Foundation — Module 3
Digital vs. Organic Intelligence: Complementary, Not Competitive
Contrasted digital intelligence (pattern recognition, speed, tireless availability) with organic intelligence (wisdom, judgment, contextual depth), arguing that each is diminished without the other.
"Digital intelligence without organic intelligence is practically useless — and the flip side is that organic intelligence is tremendously augmented by digital intelligence."
💬
Foundation — Module 4
The Art of Conversation: AI Is Not a Search Engine
Stressed that effective prompting is a narrative act — stream-of-consciousness writing carries far more semantic information than keywords. Demonstrated how domain knowledge expressed in plain language produces rich, sourced responses.
"The words you say have a high level of information content. AI picks out that meaning and applies it when it searches for you."
🖼️
Live Demo — Applied Skills
Visualization as a Practice: From Prompt to Portal
Live-built a current events trending visualization in real time — writing a narrative prompt, submitting to Claude, watching RAG fetch sources, and rendering HTML output — closing the loop between AI literacy and the portal's weekly workflow.
"That's how I produce these things for the portal every week. I just put the dates in, run this, and get this result."
