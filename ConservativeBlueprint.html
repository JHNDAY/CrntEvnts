The Conservative Promise

Project 2025: A Vision for American Governance
Educational Overview: This page presents the stated objectives and structure of Project 2025, a policy initiative developed by conservative organizations. This is an informational summary based on publicly available documents and does not constitute endorsement or criticism.
Section 1: Foundations of Government
Executive Power Reclaimed
  • Centralize control in the presidency
  • Purge or subordinate bureaucracy
  • Restore constitutional originalism
  • Limit Congress and courts' policymaking authority
  • Reclassify federal workforce under Schedule F
  • Establish presidential authority over independent agencies
⬇
Section 2: The Common Defense
National Strength as Moral Duty
  • Military readiness and deterrence first
  • Confront China; support allies conditionally
  • Restore military discipline, end "social agendas"
  • Rebuild industrial base and national sovereignty
  • Prioritize nuclear modernization
  • Reject international institutions limiting U.S. autonomy
⬇
Section 3: The General Welfare
Welfare State Rolled Back
  • Return social policy to families and states
  • Defund DEI, abortion access, and gender programs
  • Privatize and localize education and housing
  • Shrink HHS, DOJ, EPA, and Labor bureaucracy
  • Eliminate Department of Education
  • Restrict reproductive healthcare access
  • Reform entitlements toward work requirements
⬇
Section 4: The Economy
Market Freedom as Patriotism
  • Cut taxes and eliminate regulations
  • End ESG and climate mandates
  • Restore fossil energy and manufacturing
  • Reject globalism; implement "America First" trade
  • Dismantle financial regulations on businesses
  • Weaken labor union protections
  • Withdraw from climate agreements
⬇
Section 5: The American Culture
Moral Renewal as National Purpose
  • Center public life on faith and traditional family
  • Replace "woke" values with civic virtue
  • Defend religious freedom and free speech
  • Revive patriotic education and national unity
  • Restrict LGBTQ+ rights and recognition
  • Promote Christian nationalism in public policy
  • Combat "cultural Marxism" in institutions

Flow of Intent: The Strategic Sequence

1. Reassert Presidential Control — Consolidate executive authority and loyalty
2. Militarize & Fortify the State — Project strength, prepare for conflict
3. Dismantle Welfare Bureaucracy — Shrink federal safety nets, decentralize power
4. Unleash Capitalist Nationalism — Deregulate markets, prioritize domestic industry
5. Restore a Christian Moral Order — Reshape culture around traditional religious values
Source: Project 2025 is primarily coordinated by The Heritage Foundation with contributions from over 100 conservative organizations. The initiative encompasses a 900+ page policy mandate titled "Mandate for Leadership: The Conservative Promise."
