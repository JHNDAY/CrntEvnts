Timeline of International Responses

Jan 2025

Trump Orders 90-Day Aid Freeze

Executive order freezes all US foreign assistance. USAID operations begin shutting down globally.

Feb 2025

EU Acknowledges Cannot Fill Gap

European Commission officially states "EU cannot fill this gap left by others" as NGOs request emergency funding.

Mar 2025

China Announces Cambodia Projects

Within one week of US cancellation, China announces funding for identical child literacy and nutrition programs in Cambodia.

May 2025

China Launches "Small and Beautiful"

CIDCA debuts grassroots development initiative in Kyrgyzstan, marking shift from large infrastructure to community-level projects.

Jul 2025

USAID Officially Dissolved

Agency operations transfer to State Department. Study predicts 14.1 million deaths by 2030 if cuts continue.

Aug 2025

China BRI Hits $124B in H1

Belt & Road Initiative investments in first half of 2025 exceed entire previous year, with focus on Central Asia and resource-rich regions.
