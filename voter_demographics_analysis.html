üîç Methodology Notes

State Selection Criteria:

  • Hispanic Swing States: States with 15%+ Hispanic population OR 5%+ Hispanic population in competitive electoral states
  • Rust Belt States: Traditional industrial manufacturing states with significant union presence and economic transformation since 1980s
  • Young Voter Key States: States with high college-age populations, growing tech sectors, or where 18-29 demographic could influence outcomes
  • Older Voter Key States: States with 18%+ population aged 65+ OR retirement destination states with significant electoral votes

Analysis Period: Based on 2020-2024 demographic trends and 2024 electoral competitiveness assessments.
