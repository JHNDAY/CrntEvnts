October 7 Celebrations
Key Finding: Documented Street Celebrations
Multiple credible sources confirmed public celebrations in Gaza and West Bank following the October 7 attack.
MEMRI - October 29, 2023

"Palestinians in Gaza celebrated the attack in the streets just hours after Hamas invaded southern Israel. Al-Jazeera video showed celebrations in Gaza, Nablus, Jenin, and Bethlehem."

West Bank Activities
Dancing, flag waving, sweet distribution, and gunfire celebrations documented across multiple cities.
