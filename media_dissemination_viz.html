Conservative Media Dissemination Network

Direct Dissemination Hubs (Primary Sources)

1. Turning Point USA (TPUSA) & Charlie Kirk
Target: Gen Z and Millennials
Method: High-energy digital content and campus activism
Impact: Most critical source for understanding the conservative youth movement, disseminating "populist-nationalist" views directly to younger voters
2. The Daily Wire (Ben Shapiro, Matt Walsh)
Target: Under-50 conservatives
Method: Digital hub with cultural commentary
Impact: Shapiro is the most-shared conservative voice on social media, translating policy (like "biblically based" family definitions) into cultural commentary for Millennials and Gen X
3. The Heritage Foundation (Project 2025)
Focus: Policy engine
Output: Project 2025 blueprint
Impact: Strategic framework for future conservative governance with actionable policy suggestions
4. Christopher Rufo (X)
Specialty: Culture War dissemination
Method: Strategic framing and narrative architecture
Impact: Primary architect of CRT and DEI framing, demonstrating how a single influencer can shift national narrative
5. Hillsdale College (Imprimis Newsletter)
Position: Intellectual and academic center of "post-liberal" right
Focus: Constitutional originalism and education overhaul
Reach: Over 6 million subscribers
6. X (formerly Twitter) & Rumble
Key Figures: Elon Musk, Tucker Carlson
Method: Long-form interviews and unfiltered content
Impact: Bypass traditional media "gatekeepers" with direct audience engagement

"Bridge" Outlets

The Wall Street Journal (Opinion Section)
Function: Most influential "bridge" outlet
Audience: Business and political elite
Note: While news reporting is mainstream, Opinion section disseminates intellectual conservative views to broader establishment
The Dispatch or The Bulwark
Perspective: "Never-Trump" or traditional conservative
Approach: "Realistic solutions" rather than outrage
Value: Critical of current populist dissemination methods

Minnesota ICE Enforcement: Coverage by Demographic

ðŸ“… Catalyzing Event: January 23, 2026

"Day of Truth and Freedom" - A massive statewide economic strike and rally in Minnesota protesting a surge in ICE enforcement and the fatal shooting of Minneapolis mother Renee Good by an ICE officer earlier this month.

Silent & Baby Boomers (60+)
Primary Platforms: Fox News, Newsmax, Breitbart
Content Focus: National security, criminal enforcement, and safety of officers
  • Making America Safe: DHS Marks 3,000 Arrests in Operation Metro Surge
    Reports on arrest of "worst of the worst" criminal illegal aliens, including gang members and violent offenders in Minnesota
  • Radical Rhetoric Leads to 1,300% Increase in Assaults Against ICE
    High-engagement DHS report blaming "sanctuary politicians" for inciting violence and vehicular attacks against ICE agents
  • Tom Homan: 10,000 New ICE Agents Deployed by January 2026
    Coverage of Fox News interview where the "border czar" details plans to triple the size of enforcement operations
Gen X (45-60)
Primary Platforms: Fox News, X (Twitter), Facebook
Content Focus: Political conflict narratives and the "war" between federal enforcement and local sanctuary cities
  • 57 Times Unhinged Democrats Declared War on Law Enforcement
    Widely shared list of statements from Democratic leaders (Mayor Michelle Wu, Rep. Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez) used to frame current opposition as "anti-law enforcement"
  • ICE Rings in 2026 with Progress in Removing "Disgusting Monsters"
    Updates on removal of convicted sex offenders and murderers, highlighting "safer communities in 2026"
Millennials (30-45)
Primary Platforms: The Daily Wire, Substack, Reddit
Content Focus: Long-form analysis, civil liberties, and the systemic "police state" debate
  • Substack: Letters from an American - Immigration and the Suppression of Dissent
    Analysis of how the administration uses ICE sweeps and federalized National Guard troops to suppress protests
  • Reddit: The Rise of ICE as Proof of a Pro-Police State
    Viral thread on "r/ChangeMyView" debating whether recent ICE power surges represent a shift toward authoritarianism
  • Pew Research: Record 14 Million Unauthorized Immigrants in U.S.
    Frequently cited statistical anchor for debates on the scale of the enforcement task
Gen Z (18-29)
Primary Platforms: TikTok, YouTube, X, Rumble
Content Focus: Visual "receipts," personal stories of detentions, and organizational tools for activism
  • The Guardian: Minnesotans Strike to Protest ICE Surge
    Leading news report for today's "No work, no school, no shopping" blackout
  • Video: ICE Agent Tackles Child in Freezing Snow
    Viral video shared across TikTok and X showing detention of teenager yelling "I'm legal!" in Minneapolis
  • Federal Officers Detain 5-Year-Old Boy as "Bait"
    Highly circulated story about preschooler detained with his father to draw out other family members
  • TikTok Finalizes Deal to Create New U.S. Entity
    Top trending story for Gen Z users, ensuring their primary news platform remains active in the U.S.
