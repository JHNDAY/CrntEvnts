The American Fitna

In the mid-11th century, the political polity of the multi-cultural flourishing of Córdoba, "The Jewel of the World," was fractured by religious zealotry and uncompromising ideology. The Andalusian Fitna—a period of civil strife and fragmentation—saw the dissolution of the Caliphate of Córdoba into competing taifa kingdoms, each claiming legitimacy while destroying the very foundations that had made al-Andalus a beacon of learning, tolerance, and prosperity. What followed was not renewal, but the systematic dismantling of institutions that had fostered centuries of intercultural dialogue and intellectual achievement. Today, we witness echoes of this historical tragedy as democratic institutions face unprecedented assault from within.
Jan 20, 2025
Assault on Birthright Citizenship
Executive Order 14160 attempted to unilaterally reinterpret the 14th Amendment, seeking to deny citizenship to children born to undocumented parents or those on temporary visas. This frontal attack on constitutional law represents a foundational challenge to American identity itself.
Impact: Threatens the citizenship of millions and undermines 150+ years of constitutional precedent
Feb 18, 2025
Capture of Independent Agencies
Executive Order 14215 mandated that independent regulatory agencies—including the Federal Election Commission, Securities and Exchange Commission, and Federal Communications Commission—submit regulations to White House review and establish "liaison offices" under presidential control.
Impact: Destroys the independence of agencies designed to be free from political interference
March 2025
Weaponization Against Law Firms
Targeted executive orders against major law firms like Perkins Coie and Paul, Weiss, revoking security clearances, terminating government contracts, and restricting federal building access based on their diversity initiatives and representation of clients in Trump-related investigations.
Impact: Chills legal representation and attacks the independence of the legal profession
March 2025
Election Infrastructure Manipulation
Through executive orders, attempted to direct the Election Assistance Commission to mandate passport requirements for voter registration and rescind voting equipment certifications. Also granted DOGE access to sensitive voter file data despite security concerns.
Impact: Undermines election integrity and creates infrastructure for voter suppression
April 2025
Attack on Higher Education
Executive Order on Campus Accountability directed the Department of Education to withhold federal funding from universities deemed "hostile environments," targeting institutions like Harvard and Columbia. Initiated DOJ investigations and threatened tax-exempt status reviews.
Impact: Threatens academic freedom and uses federal funding as a weapon against dissent
March 2025
Invocation of Alien Enemies Act
Revived the 1798 Alien Enemies Act to deport Venezuelan migrants to El Salvador's notorious CECOT mega-prison without due process hearings. Extended deportations to conflict zones including South Sudan, circumventing normal immigration courts.
Impact: Abandons due process and creates precedent for authoritarian deportation methods
Jan 20, 2025
Dismantling of Civil Service
Mass firing of federal employees, including inspectors general and heads of independent agencies like the National Labor Relations Board and Equal Employment Opportunity Commission. Implemented Schedule F classifications to remove civil service protections.
Impact: Destroys institutional knowledge and replaces expertise with political loyalty
June 2025
Mass Denaturalization Campaign
Department of Justice memo prioritizing the revocation of citizenship from naturalized Americans through civil litigation, expanding beyond traditional cases involving war criminals to include broader categories of perceived disloyalty.
Impact: Creates a two-tiered citizenship system and weaponizes denaturalization
Jan-May 2025
Dissolution of Foreign Aid Architecture
DOGE empowered to dismantle USAID, halting HIV/AIDS prevention programs, disaster relief, and global health initiatives. Suspended refugee resettlement programs and withdrew from international humanitarian commitments established since the Marshall Plan era.
Impact: Abandons America's humanitarian leadership role established by FDR and Truman
Ongoing 2025
Surveillance State Expansion
Formalized social media screening for all immigration applicants, expanded warrantless surveillance programs, and directed federal agencies to monitor and target advocacy organizations, journalists, and political opponents through coordinated task forces.
Impact: Creates infrastructure for political repression and undermines First Amendment protections
