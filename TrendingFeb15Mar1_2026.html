NPR's Feb 27 investigation found less than 14% of nearly 400,000 ICE arrests in Trump's first year involved violent criminal offenses. ICE and DHS social media posts were found to misrepresent individuals' criminal histories — including identifying the wrong person in photos and citing arrests without convictions. The Minnesota Department of Corrections documented dozens of inaccuracies.
