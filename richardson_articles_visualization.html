üìö About Heather Cox Richardson

Heather Cox Richardson is Professor of History at Boston College and one of the most influential public historians in America. Her daily Substack newsletter, Letters from an American, has become a phenomenon with over 2.7 million subscribers, making it one of the most widely-read political analysis platforms in the United States.

Richardson specializes in the American Civil War, Reconstruction, the Gilded Age, and the American West. Her books include Democracy Awakening: Notes on the State of America (2023), How the South Won the Civil War (2020), and To Make Men Free: A History of the Republican Party (2014). She has written for the Washington Post, New York Times, and The Guardian.

Her analysis places contemporary events in historical context, drawing connections between past patterns and present challenges. She emphasizes the ongoing struggle between oligarchic and democratic visions of America, and her work has become essential reading for those seeking to understand American politics through the lens of history. All articles cited above are from her Letters from an American newsletter on Substack.
