Core Concept

John introduced a framework analyzing Trump's governance style as "The Showman's Doctrine"‚Äîgovernance through dramatic announcements designed to dominate news cycles rather than achieve policy objectives.

Key Characteristics

  • No consistency in policy execution
  • Announcements designed for immediate attention
  • Initiatives abandoned when no longer "interesting"

  • Truth secondary to narrative ("lies go twice around the world before truth puts on its trousers")

Hal noted that Trump "doesn't have any policy. He's not a politician. He just does what's interesting to him. And the minute it's not interesting, he just throws it away."

The group examined how this pattern applies across multiple initiatives: Venezuela, Greenland, Panama, and Cuba.
