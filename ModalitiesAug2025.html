Key Insights
Structured Discussion Dominance
Structured facilitated discussion consistently represents 20-32% of session time, showing the group's commitment to organized agenda-driven conversation.
Expert Knowledge Sharing
Expert presentations peaked at 29% in Session 3 (genocide law discussion) and averaged 23% across sessions, indicating high value placed on deep knowledge sharing.
Growing Innovation
Experimental/innovative activities grew from 2% to 11-15%, showing increasing comfort with new technologies like AI integration.
Social Cohesion Priority
Social maintenance activities consistently occupy 10-18% of time, demonstrating the importance of relationship building in virtual groups.
Technical Challenges Decreasing
Technical problem-solving time decreased from 11% to 3% over sessions, showing improved group familiarity with virtual meeting platforms.
