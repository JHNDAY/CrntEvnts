Response Key

Positive: Generally supportive and receptive
Mixed: Conditionally supportive with reservations
Skeptical: Doubtful or resistant

Key Insights

Major Barriers: "Take Responsibility for Repair" faces the strongest resistance across all MAGA segments, often viewed as blame or guilt-based politics.

Conditional Support: Many principles receive conditional support when framed to include their priorities (traditional Americans, religious values, anti-elite sentiment).

Trust Deficit: There's deep suspicion that these principles are coded language for progressive policies or that good-faith engagement won't be reciprocated.

Potential Bridges: "Honor Human Dignity" and aspects of "Find Strength in Difference" show the most promise for common ground, especially among Religious Conservatives and Anti-Establishment Populists.

Framing Matters: How these principles are presented and implemented would be crucial - abstract concepts are less appealing than concrete examples that include their concerns.
