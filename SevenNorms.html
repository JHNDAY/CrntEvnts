Those Seven Sacred Norms

A Political Satire • Breaking Traditions with Style

Verse 1
Those seven sacred norms that once we held so dear
Are quaint old-fashioned notions in this bold new year
Why follow precedent when you can break the mold?
Tradition's just a burden for the weak and old!
Chorus
Respect for institutions - how terribly passé!
Following the rulebook - what a dull cliché!
Constitutional limits - such a dreary bore
Why honor ancient wisdom when you could have more?
Verse 2
Transparency in government - so very out of style
Accountability's a burden that just cramps your guile
Checks and balances simply slow the whole thing down
When you could rule with tweets while wearing freedom's crown
Chorus
Judicial independence - what a tiresome thing!
Congressional oversight - it clips a leader's wing!
Separation of powers - just some dusty text
Why follow musty guidelines when you know what's next?
New Verse
You'll never find me bowing to the status quo
Or letting constitutional bubbles burst
Let others take the narrow path, I'll take the show
I cannot wait to lead where precedent won't go
With all these sacred norms dismissed
Bold and free, I've been un-cursed!
Bridge
They say these norms protected us through centuries past
But progress means we're moving far beyond that class
Who needs institutional wisdom, time-tested and true?
When breaking every boundary is the bold thing to do!
Final Chorus
Those seven sacred norms we've cast into the sea
Democratic traditions - such antiquity!
The rule of law's suggestions we can simply ignore
When power is the only thing worth fighting for!
Outro
Yes, those seven sacred norms...
Are such a dreadful bore!
