🔍 Key Insights for 2026

💡 Battle for Control

With 34 districts having margins under 5%, the 2026 midterms will be decided by razor-thin margins. The nearly even split between parties (26D vs 25R) means every competitive race matters.

🏖️ California is Key

California leads with 11 competitive districts, mostly held by Democrats. These seats could be crucial for maintaining Democratic control or enabling a Republican takeover.

🆕 Freshman Vulnerability

8 freshman members are in competitive districts, making them potentially vulnerable as they haven't had time to build strong incumbent advantages.

🎯 Geographic Spread

Competitive districts span 37 states, indicating nationalized political trends will be crucial. Local factors may matter less than national political environment.
