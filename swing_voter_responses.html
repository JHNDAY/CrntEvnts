Key Insights

Broad Appeal: The pluralism principles generally resonate across these voter segments, with "Honor Human Dignity" and "Find Strength in Difference" receiving the most positive responses.

Implementation Concerns: While supportive of the concepts, these voters often want to see concrete examples and assurance that their own communities and values won't be marginalized in the process.

Common Ground: There's significant potential for building bridges through these principles, especially when framed around practical problem-solving rather than abstract ideological goals.
