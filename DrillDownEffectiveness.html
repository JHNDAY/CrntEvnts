Visual Overview Strategy

  • Cognitive Mapping

  • Democratic Prioritization

Drill-Down Methodology
"The information that's being on the screen comes from curated sources. It's not being generated by AI. But rather, based upon the context that George provides me, AI goes out and finds a whole bunch of articles"
— John Day
  • Structured Deep Dives
    Each topic provided curated facts, not just opinions
  • Source Transparency
    Live links to original sources built credibility
  • Human-AI Collaboration
    Combined contextual guidance with research efficiency

🚀 Innovation Factor

This represents a sophisticated evolution of political discussion formats, combining AI research capabilities with thoughtful information design and human facilitation. The transparency of sources and methodology addressed potential concerns about AI bias while leveraging its research efficiency.

Key Innovation: Technology enhancing rather than replacing human judgment in complex political discourse through proper integration with good facilitation and clear information architecture.
⚠️ Observed Limitations
Learning Curve: Some participants needed explanation of the interface (Ron's questions about source selection showed initial confusion)
Technical Dependencies: System required both AI integration and live web access, creating potential failure points
Source Bias Concerns: Required active management of diverse perspective inclusion
