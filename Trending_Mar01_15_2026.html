Learning Resources v. Trump — IEEPA Tariffs Struck Down (Feb 20)
The Supreme Court's February 20 ruling in Learning Resources v. Trump was a historic blow: the Court held that IEEPA had never before been used to justify unilateral executive tariffs and that the president's sweeping tariff authority under IEEPA was unconstitutional. CBP admitted it could not comply with the Court's order to process refunds. The White House said it would appeal — yet indicated it would proceed with tariff negotiations "assuming" eventual victory. Slate's legal reporters called it "one of the most significant executive power rulings of the decade."
