The Nature of the Next American Cycle

Friedman argues that America's next cycle will be defined by demographic challenges. With life expectancy at 78 years and birth rates at 1.7 children per woman, the U.S. faces a crisis where consumption rises as the productive workforce shrinks. The solution lies in medical innovation and material science to extend productive life, rather than relying solely on AI.

üìö About George Friedman

George Friedman is an internationally recognized geopolitical forecaster and the founder and chairman of Geopolitical Futures. A New York Times bestselling author, his books include The Storm Before the Calm (2020), The Next 100 Years (2009), and The Next Decade (2011). Prior to founding Geopolitical Futures, he was chairman of Stratfor, a geopolitical intelligence platform.

His analysis focuses on long-term historical cycles, geography's role in shaping national interests, and the structural forces that drive geopolitical change. All articles cited above are published on Geopolitical Futures (geopoliticalfutures.com).
