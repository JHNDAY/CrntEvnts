Survival Strategies
💪 "Rage Giving" Success Stories
Louisville Public Media raised $525,000 in 4 days, gaining 1,000 new members. NH Public Radio raised over $1M through car raffles and major gifts. Even nuns who took vows of poverty donated.
🤝 Unprecedented Coordination
Wisconsin public media leaders held their first-ever collaboration meeting. Stations are sharing back-end services and operations like never before.
🏗️ Infrastructure Takeover
NPR negotiating with PBS to handle music rights and distribution services previously managed by CPB, ensuring continuity.
💰 NPR Fee Relief Program
NPR offering $8M in relief to stations that relied on federal funding for 10%+ of their budgets - direct financial support.
🌱 Bottom-Up Innovation
Station leaders advocate for local, community-centered solutions. "You can't fill a $535 million gap with incremental change."
Rally Cry: New Hampshire Public Radio has adopted "onward, dammit" - a phrase spreading across the network as stations fight for survival.
