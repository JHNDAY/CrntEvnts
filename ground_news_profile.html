Core Mission

1. Comprehension not Consumption

Keep you informed, not addicted. No rabbit holes, just diverse perspectives.

2. Subscription not Ads

No clickbait or sensationalism. Ad-free platform funded by subscribers.

3. Pragmatic not Partisan

No censorship of news sources. All perspectives as long as they cover facts.
